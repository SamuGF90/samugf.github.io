Transient absorption of 2D perovskites

MSc PhD

We study ultrafast charge dynamics in layered perovskites using fs/ns TAS. You will learn sample prep, measurement, and analysis workflows.

ELTE

Aims

  • Measure transient absorption of Ruddlesden–Popper perovskites.
  • Extract carrier lifetimes and exciton binding energies.
  • Correlate morphology with photophysics.

Methods

Ultrafast pump–probe spectroscopy (fs/ns), PL, sample fabrication, and numerical fitting.

What you’ll learn

  • Ultrafast optical techniques
  • Data analysis and modeling
  • Scientific writing and presentation
